**TARA ROTH** is the president of the Goldhirsh Foundation. Prior to this role, Tara bridged the worlds of marketing, media, and philanthropy as the founding COO of [GOOD](http://GOOD.is) and a social strategy advisor. Tara began her career in marketing and business development for NBCinternet and Infoseek. She has worked with organizations such as [Participant Media](http://www.participantmedia.com/), [Fifteen Foundation](http://www.15foundation.org/), and the [New Schools Venture Fund](http://www.newschools.org/). As a senior advisor to [GOOD](http://GOOD.is), Tara helped launch the Pepsi Refresh Project.
